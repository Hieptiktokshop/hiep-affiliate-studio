Hiep Affiliate Studio

Hiep Affiliate Studio is a content preparation and workflow management tool designed to support TikTok Affiliate creators.

What the system helps with

  • Organize affiliate products manually
  • Prepare content ideas, captions, and hashtags
  • Review and control content before publishing

Important Notice

Hiep Affiliate Studio does NOT automatically post videos to TikTok, does NOT perform mass posting, and does NOT engage in spam or automated publishing.

All final content is reviewed and posted manually by the creator.
